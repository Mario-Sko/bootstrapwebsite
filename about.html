Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival was found in 2016. It is a beautiful festival and one of Brooklyn's most popular annual cultural events. Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is a local, national and international celebration of cinema. It is running annually in August. The Festival showcases the latest and best international and national filmes, documentaries and shorts.
