Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is a beautiful festival and one of Brooklyn's most popular annual cultural events. Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is a local, national and international celebration of cinema. It is running annually in August. The Festival showcases the latest and best international and national filmes, documentaries and shorts. If you attended the 2019 Festival, we would like to thank you for your support and we look forward to welcoming you back in 2020.
